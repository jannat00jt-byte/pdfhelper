Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Introduction

PDFHelper Tool ("we", "our" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our online PDF conversion tools.

2. Data we collect

We believe in minimal data collection:

  • Processed files: Your files are processed exclusively locally in your browser. No files are uploaded, stored or transmitted to our servers.

  • Advertising: We use third-party ad networks (Adsterra, Google AdSense) which may use cookies for relevant ad delivery.

3. How we use your data

  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Analyze site performance
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies

We use essential, analytical and advertising cookies. You can control cookie usage through your browser settings.

5. Data security

Your files are processed locally in your browser and never leave your device. We use HTTPS encryption for all communications.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to processing, and data portability.

For questions about this policy, contact us at our contact page or email: contact@pdftools.com
